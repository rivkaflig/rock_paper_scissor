Rock Paper Scissors

Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors to play against the computer

Welcome to the exciting world of Rock, Paper, Scissors! In this fun game, you’ll be facing off against the computer to see who’s the ultimate champion. Here’s how it works:
You’ll choose one of three shapes with your hand:

Rock (a mighty fist!): Crushes Scissors
Scissors (snip snip!): Cut through Paper
Paper (a smooth open hand!): Covers up Rock

If you both choose the same shape, it’s a tie—but don’t worry, you can always try again! Whether you’re here for a quick decision or just a fun challenge, Rock, Paper, Scissors is the perfect game to keep things lighthearted and entertaining. Ready to take on the computer? Let’s see who wins! 👊🖐️✌️
